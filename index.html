BARIATRIC SURGERY QUALIFICATION INFO

BMI's above 30 may qualify you for the Gastric Balloon.

BMI's above 35 may qualify you for any weight loss surgery procedure if you also have poorly controlled type 2 disbetes, have a higher risk of caediovascular disease, or suffer from another weight-related health issue.

BMI's above 40may qualify you for any weight loss surgery procedure other than the Gastric Balloon, regardless of existing health issues.

BMI's above 45 may qualify you for any weight loss surgery procedure other than the Gastric Balloon or Bloc Therapy, regardless of existing health issues.
